Hana Shirosaki

Hana Shirosaki is one of the main characters from the Watashi ni Tenshi ga Maiorita! series. She is the only daughter of Haruka Shirosaki and is one of Hinata Hoshino's friends. She is usually seen in her school uniform, which is a long navy blue dress with a red bow, white sleeves, a navy blue striped collar, white tights and brown shoes.
  • Native Name: 白咲花
  • Birth: 7 Maret 2006
  • Zodiac: Pisces
  • Height: 142cm
  • Weight: 38kg
  • Food: Pudding
